About the Global Rotation Gateway

Our Mission

The Global Rotation Gateway is dedicated to connecting medical students with enriching international rotation opportunities. We believe that global health experiences are essential for developing well-rounded, culturally competent physicians.

Why We Exist

Finding reliable and up-to-date information on international rotations can be a daunting task. Our platform aims to simplify this process by providing a centralized, comprehensive resource for medical students seeking global health opportunities.

Our Team

We are a team of medical professionals, educators, and technology enthusiasts passionate about global health and medical education. Our diverse backgrounds and shared vision drive us to create a valuable resource for the next generation of physicians.

Partnerships

We collaborate with medical associations, universities, and healthcare organizations worldwide to ensure the quality and accuracy of our rotation listings. We are always looking to expand our network of partners to further our mission.
