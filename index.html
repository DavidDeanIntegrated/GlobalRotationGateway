The Tourist Visa Trap
Using a tourist visa to complete clinical rotations is illegal in several key countries covered on this site. In India, a student visa is mandatory for all clinical floors â€” tourists found on wards face deportation. South Africa requires HPCSA registration, which functions as a temporary clinical license. Thailand (Ramathibodi) requires a Thai Non-Immigrant Education (ED) Visa from a Royal Thai Embassy before travel â€” entering on a tourist visa is explicitly not legally permitted. The Health Professions Council of South Africa (HPCSA) registration is mandatory for all clinical rotations in South Africa.
